Air Fryer Hoisin Glazed Chicken Thighs

This quick and easy Air Fryer Hoisin Glazed Chicken Thigh recipe is the perfect meal after a long day. It comes together in minutes, and can be prepped up to a day in advance. The prep time is roughly 15 minutes, but we recommend marinating a day in advance, with a cook time of 15 minutes as well. This recipe yields 4 services.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup hoisin sauce
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 2 teaspoons toasted sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon Sriracha
  • 2 cloves garlic, grated
  • 2 teaspoons grated ginger
  • 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • Cooked rice
  • Sesame seeds to garnish
  • Green onion to garnish

Directions

  1. Whisk together hoisin sauce, maple syrup, sesame oil, sriracha, garlic and ginger until well combined.
  2. Pour 2/3 of mixture over the chicken thighs and toss to coat. Let marinate for a minimum of 30 minutes (or up to 24 hours).
  3. Preheat the air fryer to 375Â°F. Add the chicken thighs in a single layer and cook for 7-8 minutes on each side. Brush with remaining sauce when you flip over the chicken.
  4. Garnish with sesame seeds and green onion. Plate over rice, and serve.
